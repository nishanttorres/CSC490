Livingston boss set for sleepless night after dismal display at St Mirren:
In this article, Livingston boss David Martindale reflects on his team's dismal display as they were soundly beaten 3-0 at St Mirren. He acknowledges that the entire squad and coaching staff will likely have a sleepless night trying to come to terms with the defeat.
